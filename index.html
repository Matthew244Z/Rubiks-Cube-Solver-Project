This is a Rubik's Cube solver by layer. It tells you all the steps and moves are necessary to solve the cube.


The cube in the left shows the move you need to make.

The cube in the right shows how your cube should be.

Use the buttons to control the moves or click directly on the steps to see them.

Go to moves if you want to learn the terminologies (you don't need to). You only need to know that 2 means that you move the layer twice.


Go to another rubik's cube webiste and copy the scramble formula or use the 'Generate Scramble!' button.
